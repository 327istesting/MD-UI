Why Meta Defender is for you?

Supercharged Yields


Enable yield tokens such as Pendle’s YT to be traded in perpetual markets and achieve leveraged returns via meta-yield token standard (metaYT) that captures both realized and unrealized yields.

Derivatives Marketplace for Yield Trading

Yields fluctuate by nature. Meta Defender offers a decentralized derivatives marketplace purposely built with deep liquidity to mitigate and speculate yields, and multiple returns.

Capital Efficient


Meta Defender’s derivatives market redeploys a portion of its LP into yield markets to dramatically enhance capital efficiency while ensuring capital availability with mechanisms like cooldown period and dynamic gauge to adjust the redeployable ratio.

Stacked Rewards

Meta Defender’s redeployable LPs can be integrated into yield markets and yield boosters to enable Meta Defender LPs earn extra fees and rewards along the stack.

Bank-like Fixed-yield Experience

Meta Defender Earn offers a real fixed-yield product without lockups similar to the experience of a checking account in a bank using Pendle’s PT token and metaYT token in its perpetual market to achieve yield stability despite market volatility.

Multi-Chain by Design


Meta Defender will be launched on Ethereum first, then on Arbitrum and Acala to continue improve user experience, and expand quality LST asset options.

How can I earn $DEF tokens?



metaYT denotes the date it is launched and the maturity date of the underlying YT, representing both “realised” yield (from YT held by metaYT) and “unrealised” yield.
Say there is a Pendle YT with stETH as underlying LST. We launch a metaYT for this YT:
On launch 1 metaYT = 1 YT
The next day, 1 metaYT = 1 YT + yield earned on the day
…
Hence metaYT = YT + yield earned from launch to maturity.
This is critical because it normalizes metaYT where its price fluctuates with daily yield rates, making it tradable in Meta Defender perpetual trading marketplace.

How does Meta Defender Earn achieve fixed yield?



Everytime there is a deposit to Meta Defender Earn, the protocol will use a portion of the deposit to purchase Pendle’s Principal Token (PT), while simultaneously opening a Long metaYT position.
PT price decrease always corresponds with YT price increase, and vice versa. Therefore if PT price falls, the long metaYT position will make a profit to compensate it perfectly to provide the set APY, for as long as the user’s willing to hold the position. Given those features, Meta Defender provides the fixed yield product with no lockup period.

What are the advantages of being a Meta Defender LP?



A Meta Defender LP can enjoy income from Meta Defender's protocol (including Funding fee, Position fee, Liquidation gains and etc.). Also, Meta Defender will allocate a portion of the idle Market Maker funds into Pendle swap via the Penpie/Equilibria, becoming an LP of Pendle, earning Pendle rewards (including swap fees, PENDLE token incentives, 3% YT yield and etc.), as well as sharing token incentives from Penpie/Equilibria.
