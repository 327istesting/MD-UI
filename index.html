Frequently Asked Questions

If you have other questions, feel free to reach out to our product team.

How do I get cover?



Covers are available for purchase by users from the application interface using a Metamask account. We are making the process as simple as possible as following steps:
1) Specify which smart contract address you want cover for;
2) Specify the covered amount;
3) Generate a quote and make the transaction using Metamask;
4) You are now covered!

How to become an underwriter?



Select the projects you are interested in on the Meta Defender website and stake stable coin as the underwriting capital in the Capital Reserve. The underwriting capital has no lock-up period, and it can enter and exit freely.

How does the underwriter withdraw liquidity?



After providing stable coins to the Capital Reserve, the underwriter can obtain NFT as a capital certificate. The NFT can be burnt at any time on Meta Defender protocol, and get back with corresponding stable coins step by step. For more details, please read our whitepaper.
