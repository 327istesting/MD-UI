Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers now

Has Meta Defender issued its own tokens yet?

Meta Defender has not yet issued our tokens. All insurance-related payments will be settled with stablecoins.

Are there careers available?

We will continue to recruit developers and marketers with outstanding capabilities worldwide. Feel free to reach out to us if you want to be a part to re-invent Web3.0 insurance!

How can I stay updated with Meta Defender's latest news and developments?

Stay tuned to our Twitter and Medium for the latest information, or join our Discord and Telegram to stay in touch with the group and address any questions you encounter.

Which blockchains does Meta Defender support?

We're currently operating on the Arbitrum Georli testnet. In the future, we will officially launch on the Ethereum mainnet and Arbitrum One.

Do I need special skills or knowledge to use Meta Defender?

While some understanding of blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies can be helpful, Meta Defender is designed to be user-friendly and accessible. We provide a range of resources to help users understand and make the most of our platform, and our community is always ready to assist with any questions.

How does Meta Defender handle security?

We take security very seriously at Meta Defender. Our smart contracts are audited by top security firms, and we implement strict security protocols in our operations. We also encourage users to follow best practices for digital security and asset management.
